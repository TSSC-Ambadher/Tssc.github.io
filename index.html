SMS Parent App

The Standard School and College, located in Ambadher, Charsadda, and founded by Aman ul Haq, integrates a cutting-edge Smart School System with a dedicated Parent App, available on Android and iOS, to enhance the educational experience for both male and female students from Nursery to 2nd Year. How SMS Parents works

At The Standard School and College in Ambadher, Charsadda, founded by Aman ul Haq, registering for our Smart School System and Parent App is straightforward. Parents receive a unique registration code upon student enrollment, download the app on Android or iOS, and create an account using the code, their contact details, and the student’s ID. After school verification, parents gain instant access to track attendance, marks, homework, and notifications for their child (Nursery to 2nd Year, male and female). New students register during admission, submitting required documents at the campus. For support, contact our school office.
